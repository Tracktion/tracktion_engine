◆ fixedBlockSize

bool tracktion_engine::HostedAudioDeviceInterface::Parameters::fixedBlockSize = false

If the size of the audio buffer passed to processBlock will be fixed or not.

If you are creating a plugin, this should be false, and your plguin will have one block of latency. If you are handling the audio device callback yourself, this can be true.
